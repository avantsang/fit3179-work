Yearly Count of Space Missions Across the World Over Time

Motivated by the Space Race between Russia and the USA, 1955-1975 was the golden age of spaceflight development, and thus saw the most and earliest activity in pursuit of the revolutionary technology that is spaceflight.

Over time, space flight garnered international interest by countries such as Australia, India and Korea, but with little funding, so was their success.

China however, has taken the world by storm and has mad massive success in spaceflight after committing more funds to their aerospace program in 2016.
